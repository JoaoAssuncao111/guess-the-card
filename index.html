TUTORIAL

The goal of the game is to guess the card through sheer luck.

Step 1: Guess the Suit's color

Step 2: Guess the Card's Suit

Step 3: Guess if it's a Number or a Figure

Step 3.5: (Numbers only) Guess if it's lower or equal to 5, or greater than 5

Step 4: Guess the exact Figure or Number

If you miss any guess you'll start over on a new card and you gain 1 Point by completing Steps 1 to 4

When a new card is drawn, the previous one is added to the History

You can hover the deck to see how many cards there are left

Good Luck!
